Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

If you have any additional questions or need further assistance, feel free to contact our customer support team through the contact information provided on our website.

How does Lucky Tree Raffle work?

Lucky Tree Raffle is an online platform where you can participate in raffles to win exciting prizes. Simply register on our website, purchase raffle tickets, and wait for the drawing. If you win, you'll be notified and receive the prize!

How can I participate in a raffle?

To participate, you need to create an account on our website, browse the available raffles, and purchase raffle tickets for the desired raffle. Each raffle may have different ticket prices and available quantities.

Can I buy multiple raffle tickets?

Yes, you can buy multiple raffle tickets for any given raffle. The more tickets you purchase, the higher your chances of winning.

How are the winners selected?

Winners are selected through a random drawing process conducted on a specified date and time. Our system ensures fairness and transparency in the selection.

How will I know if I win?

If you win a raffle, we will notify you via email or phone within a reasonable timeframe after the drawing. Make sure to keep your contact information up to date in your account settings.

What happens if I don't claim my prize?

Winners are typically given a specific timeframe to claim their prize. If a winner fails to claim the prize within that timeframe, it may be forfeited, and an alternate winner may be selected.
